# Problem Statement: Smart Resume Analyzer

## Overview
Job seekers often struggle to tailor their resumes for specific job postings,
leading to rejection by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) before a human even
reads their application. Studies show that over 75% of resumes are rejected by
ATS before reaching a recruiter.

## Problem
There is no easy, automated way for candidates to:
1. Understand how well their resume matches a specific job description
2. Identify which important keywords are missing from their resume
3. Get specific, actionable suggestions on what to add/change

## Solution
A multi-agent AI system (Smart Resume Analyzer) that:
- Parses and structures resume content
- Deeply analyzes job description requirements
- Calculates a keyword match score
- Identifies gaps and missing keywords
- Generates a professional improvement report

## Target Users
Job seekers at all levels looking to improve their resume-to-JD alignment.

## Success Criteria
- Match score calculated accurately
- At least 10 specific keyword recommendations provided
- Full markdown report generated within one interaction
